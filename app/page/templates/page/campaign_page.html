Who We Serve

Fairhill School is a community dedicated to educating, engaging, and inspiring students who have learning differences. Our primary purpose is to provide a superior education for students who have been diagnosed with a learning difference such as Dyslexia, Dysgraphia, Dyscalculia, Auditory Processing Disorder, or Attention Deficit/Hyperactivity Disorder. Fairhill students possess the intellectual potential to succeed academically, often at superior levels. However, because of a learning difference, these students have frequently experienced frustration in traditional school environments.

At Fairhill we offer a warm, supportive atmosphere where these bright children have the opportunity to thrive. Our student-centered curriculum focuses on differentiation, multi-sensory experiences, executive function development, and intervention for individualized learning needs. As a private, non-profit, college preparatory school serving grades 1-12, Fairhill fosters each studentâ€™s unique potential while guiding each one on their journey toward their dreams.

The Fairhill Experience

At Fairhill each student is offered a unique approach to traditional education. Our small student to teacher ratios facilitate a supportive and attentive environment. Our expansive campus is set on sixteen wooded acres and includes an athletic field, playground, nature-study area, technology labs, a library/media center, a Fine Arts wing, our Diagnostic Assessment Center, a gymnasium/multipurpose facility, and tennis courts. Our Diagnostic Assessment Center was developed to establish an inventory of the talents and skills unique to each individual. These assessments provide educators the necessary information to offer appropriate classroom accommodations and modifications to help each student achieve academic and social success.

Additionally, our academic programs are complemented by enrichment classes-+ including art, music, drama, computer applications, robotics, library science, executive functions skills, and communication skills; with the addition of honors programs and further electives in grades 9-12.

College and Career Readiness

Our college prep course is designed to prepare students for their senior year and academic life after high school. With a dominant focus on college prep, it also takes into consideration alternate career and educational opportunities.

Senior Buddy Program

Members of the Senior Class who choose to participate are paired with a first, second, or third grade student to share scheduled learning activities once a month. The Buddy Program provides an avenue for younger and older students to develop strong bonds of friendship.

Activities

Fairhill offers extracurricular activities that inspire students, building self-esteem, creating confidence, encouraging teamwork, and promoting responsibility. While our main focus is on academics, emphasis is also placed on sports, the arts, and social responsibility.
